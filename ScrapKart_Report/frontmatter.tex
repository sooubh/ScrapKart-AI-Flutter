\chapter*{ABSTRACT}
\thispagestyle{empty}

\vspace{1cm}
\setstretch{1}
\hyphenpenalty=10000

The traditional scrap collection and recycling process relies heavily on informal sectors, leading to inefficiencies, lack of transparency in pricing, and disjointed communication between households and scrap collectors. With increasing urbanization and waste generation, it is imperative to establish a structured and intelligent waste management ecosystem that promotes sustainability and maximizes recycling rates. Conventional methods lack standardized mechanisms for real-time scrap evaluation, dynamic pricing, and optimized routing for collectors, causing delays and resource wastage.\\

\noindent The proposed system, ScrapKart AI, is an intelligent web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between consumers looking to sell their recyclable waste and verified scrap collectors. By integrating modern web technologies such as Next.js, Node.js, and MongoDB with advanced Artificial Intelligence models like Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and YOLO (You Only Look Once), the system provides a seamless experience for waste disposal. Users can upload images of their scrap materials, which the AI engine automatically classifies to estimate their current market value based on real-time trends. Furthermore, the platform incorporates a robust price prediction engine and leverages route optimization algorithms via Google Maps API to assist collectors in scheduling efficient pickups.\\

\noindent Utilizing an AI-driven approach significantly minimizes human error in scrap evaluation, ensures fair pricing, and improves operational efficiency for collectors by providing optimized travel paths. ScrapKart AI also features a dedicated sustainability analytics module that tracks the environmental impact of recycled items, providing users with insights into their carbon footprint reduction.\\

\noindent In conclusion, ScrapKart AI transforms the unorganized scrap market into a digitized, transparent, and eco-friendly framework. It represents a significant step towards smart waste management and sustainable living, empowering communities to actively participate in recycling while receiving fair compensation for their contributions.\\

\noindent Keywords: Artificial Intelligence, Smart Waste Management, Convolutional Neural Networks, Pricing Prediction, Route Optimization, Web Application, Sustainability.
